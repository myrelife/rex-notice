九年的守護，碎心的告別：RE·X 營運止步與轉讓公告 A Heartfelt Farewell: Notice of Operational Suspension and Strategic Business Transfer for RE·X

致 最親愛的合作店家與用戶朋友們：

這是一封我們掙扎許久、忍著劇痛才寫下的信

如果可以，我們多麼希望 RE·X（RE紅包）的故事能一直寫下去。這九年來，我們與各位並肩度過了無數挑戰。從最初一個微小的創意點子，到成為一個服務成千上萬用戶與店家的平台，RE·X 對我們而言，不只是一間公司，更是一個承載著夢想與承諾的孩子

這一次，我們真的已經傾盡所有。在過去這段艱難的日子裡，受限於劇烈動盪的地緣政治環境，原本預計的募資計劃遭到重挫。為了守住對大家的承諾，我們用盡了最後一分資金，甚至不惜負債累累，只求能撐過寒冬。直到今天，我們不得不面對殘酷的現實——RE·X 的財務能量已燃燒殆盡，我們必須沈痛地宣佈：即日起，RE·X 將停止一切現有營運

對不起，讓大家失望了。想到這個消息會帶給各位的震驚與不解，我們感到萬分心碎與慚愧。我們深知，這背後是無數店家的信任，以及廣大用戶對我們的支持。對於沒能陪各位走得更遠，造成大家的困擾與損害，我們除了深深一鞠躬，心中滿是無法言喻的歉疚

最後的期盼：尋求強大的羽翼，守護各位的權益。雖然 RE·X 現階段的營運被迫止步，但這套累積九年的技術系統與營運成果，是我們最珍貴的資產。我們不甘心這一切付諸流水，更不忍心讓大家的權益受損

因此，我們正積極尋求各界有實力的集團公司進行「營業讓與」或「策略性併購」。我們願意以最開放、最低的姿態，尋求強而有力的企業收購接手

這不僅是 RE·X 的重生機會，更是我們守護大家權益的最後希望。若能有更強大的羽翼願意承接，讓平台得以延續，這將是我們在黑暗中唯一能感到的慰藉

最後的請求　在這個最痛苦的時刻，懇請各位店家與用戶能給予我們一點體諒。我們正在與時間賽跑，接洽所有可能的收購方，只為了能給各位一個更好的交代

感謝這九年來生命中有你們。對於這份沉重的抱歉，我們會用盡餘力去尋求轉機

To our most cherished partner stores and users,

This is a letter we have struggled to write, and we do so today with a truly heavy heart.

If it were at all possible, we would have given anything to keep the story of RE·X going. Over the past 9 years of shared journey — we have stood side-by-side with you to overcome countless challenges. From a small, innovative idea to a platform serving thousands of businesses and users, RE·X has never been just a company to us; it has been a dream we nurtured with every ounce of our being.

We have exhausted every resource to keep this promise. In recent times, faced with an unprecedented and volatile geopolitical environment, our planned fundraising efforts were severely disrupted. To honor our commitments to you, we exhausted every bit of available capital and took on a significant financial burden, desperately hoping to weather this storm. Regrettably, we have reached a point where our financial energy is entirely depleted. It is with profound sadness that we must announce: Effective immediately, RE·X will suspend all current operations.

We are profoundly sorry for failing you. The thought of the shock and confusion this news will cause you breaks our hearts. We are deeply aware that behind RE·X lies the trust of countless partner stores and the loyal support of our users. For our inability to carry this mission further, and for the distress and inconvenience this causes, we offer our most sincere and humble apologies.

Our Final Hope: Seeking a Stronger Wing to Protect Your Interests. While our current operations must come to a halt, the technological architecture and the extensive service network we have built over 9 years remain our most valuable assets. We cannot bear to see these efforts vanish, nor can we endure the thought of your interests being left unprotected.

Therefore, we are actively and openly seeking opportunities for a “Business Transfer” or “Strategic Acquisition.” We are reaching out to robust corporate groups with a humble and open mind, hoping to find a partner to acquire and take over the RE·X platform.

This is not only a chance for RE·X to be reborn but, more importantly, it is our final hope to safeguard your rights and interests. If a stronger entity can take the lead and allow our services to continue, it would be the only solace we can find in this dark hour.

A Final Request — In this incredibly painful time, we humbly ask for your understanding. We are racing against the clock to contact all potential acquirers, driven solely by the desire to provide a better resolution for everyone involved.

Thank you for being part of our lives for these 9 years. We will dedicate every remaining effort to seeking a turning point for this transition.

With deepest gratitude and sincerest apologies,

The RE·X Management Team
